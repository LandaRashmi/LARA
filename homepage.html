LARA is a structured documentation system designed for legal research and AI development. It facilitates efficient data acquisition, search functionality, and resource management. By integrating advanced tools, LARA enhances accuracy, accessibility, and organization, streamlining research and development workflows for professionals and researchers.

Qlara

The Chatbot in LARA delivers AI-powered legal assistance, answering queries on laws, judgments, terms, and case references with speed and accuracy. It offers detailed explanations, insights, and cross-references, ensuring reliable responses for researchers and practitioners.

Acts & Sections

Explore 890 acts and sections with an easy-to-use search bar. Find specific acts or provisions and view detailed information, including definitions, chapters, and footnotes. You can download sections for offline use, streamlining your legal research.

Articles

The Articles section in LARA allows users to search and reference constitutional articles, providing quick access to legal provisions, interpretations, and related case laws. It includes detailed explanations, cross-references, and highlighted keywords for better navigation.

Legal Glossary

The Legal Glossary in LARA provides definitions and explanations of legal terms, ensuring clarity and understanding of complex concepts for researchers, professionals, and students. It includes references, examples, and highlighted keywords for better navigation and learning.

Judgements

The Judgments section in LARA allows users to search and view case judgments, providing access to legal precedents, references, and key details like plaintiff vs. defendant, judgment dates, and judge names, with highlighted keywords for easy navigation.

Templates

The Document Templates section in LARA offers a collection of preformatted legal templates for contracts, agreements, petitions, and more. It helps users draft legal documents efficiently with structured formats, editable fields, and guidelines, ensuring consistency and professionalism.
